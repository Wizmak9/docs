Welcome to NextAI, your go-to platform for seamless AI service management. This guide will walk you through the process of logging into your account, navigating to your profile, adding a new service account, and obtaining service account credentials for GCP and AWS.

## **Step 1: Logging In**

1. Visit [https://app.nextai.co.in](https://app.nextai.co.in/) to access the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address and password in the provided fields.
3. Click on the "Login" button to access your dashboard.

## **Step 2: Navigating to Your Profile**

1. Once logged in, locate the user profile icon at the top left corner of the dashboard.
2. Click on the user profile icon to open a dropdown menu.
3. Select "Profile" from the dropdown options to access your user profile page.

## **Step 3: Adding a Service Account**

1. In your profile page, you will find the "Add Service Account" option.
2. Click on "Add Service Account" to open the service account form.
3. Fill in the form with the following details:
    - **Name of the Service Account:** Provide a unique name for easy identification.
    - **Service Provider:** Select the service provider for your account (GCP or AWS).
    - **Credentials:** Enter your service provider credentials in JSON format.

## **Step 4: Obtaining Service Account Credentials**

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="For Google Cloud Platform (GCP)">
To obtain GCP service account credentials:

1. Go to the Google Cloud Console.
2. Navigate to "IAM & Admin" > "Service Accounts."
3. Click on "Create Service Account" and follow the prompts to set up a new service account.
4. Create a role following this [link](https://docs.nextai.co.in/NextAI%20Compute/GCP%20Configurations) or give as owner to skip creating roles.
5. Once the service account is created, click on "Actions" > "Manage Key and Add key."
6. Choose "JSON" as the key type and click "Create" to download the JSON key file.
7. Use this JSON key file in the NextAI service account form under the "Credentials" field.
8. NextAI will validate the key, ensuring it has all necessary permissions. If the validation is successful, we are ready to deploy services..
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="For Amazon Web Services (AWS)">
To obtain AWS service account details:

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console.
2. Navigate to "IAM" > "Users."
3. Click on "Create User" and follow the prompts to create a new IAM user.
4. During the user creation process, be sure to grant the necessary permissions for NextAI to access AWS services to setup this follow [AWS configuration](https://docs.nextai.co.in/NextAI%20Compute/AWS%20Configurations).
5. Once a user is created, an "Access Key ID" and a "Secret Access Key" will be provided. If you already have a role, please click on the user and generate a key for third-party access.
6. Use these details in the NextAI service account form under the "Credentials" field.
7. NextAI will validate the key, ensuring all necessary permissions are in place. If the validation is successful, we are ready to deploy services.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## **Conclusion**

By following these steps, you can easily add a service account to your NextAI profile and obtain the necessary credentials from GCP or AWS. This will enable you to seamlessly manage and integrate AI services with your NextAI account.

If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
